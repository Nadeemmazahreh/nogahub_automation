NogaHub · Void Quotation Guide Overview
What is Void?

Overview

Void Acoustics Research Ltd. is a UK-based professional audio equipment manufacturer. In the NogaHub system, Void is a dedicated equipment category (category: "void") whose items are sourced in USD, imported into Jordan, and re-sold to clients in JOD through a project-based quoting workflow.

Each piece of Void equipment carries two USD prices in the database:

Dealer Price (dealerUSD)
Wholesale
What NogaHub pays Void. Used for cost, profit, and shipping/customs allocation.
MSRP (msrpUSD)
List Price
Manufacturer suggested retail price. Used as the base for the client-facing BOQ price.
Weight (kg)
Per Unit
Physical weight used to calculate proportional shipping costs per item.
Key principle: The client never sees dealer cost. The BOQ (Bill of Quantities) is always built on MSRP, with shipping and customs costs distributed on top.
The Full Pipeline at a Glance

Calculation Pipeline — 7 Stages

  1. Currency Conversion
    All USD prices are converted to JOD using a fixed exchange rate of 0.71 JOD / 1 USD.
  2. Shipping Cost Calculation
    Total weight of all items drives a per-kg shipping cost, plus fixed logistics fees (clearance, transport, delivery order).
  3. Jordanian Customs & Taxes
    Eight separate customs taxes (001, 020, 215, 301, 111, 016, 019, 070) are applied to the shipment value. Two cost variants are computed — one including tax 020, one excluding it — to avoid double-counting in the BOQ.
  4. Per-Unit BOQ Pricing
    Shipping and customs costs (excluding tax 020) are distributed proportionally across each item based on its share of the total dealer cost. The client unit price = MSRP (JOD) + allocated shipping + allocated customs.
  5. Services
    Four standard services — Commissioning, Noise Control, Sound Design, and Project Management — are added. Each has a default formula or can be overridden with a custom value.
  6. Discount
    A global discount percentage (if set on the project) is applied to the combined equipment + services subtotal.
  7. VAT (16%)
    If includeTax is enabled on the project, 16% Jordanian VAT is applied to the discounted subtotal to produce the Final Quotation Total.

NogaHub · Void Quotation Guide Stages 1–3: Currency · Shipping · Customs
Stage 1

Currency Conversion (USD → JOD)

All Void equipment prices stored in the database are in USD. Before any calculation the system converts them to JOD using a fixed exchange rate:

// Stage 1 — Currency conversion (src/config/constants.js)
EXCHANGE_RATE = 0.71 // JOD per USD

dealerJOD = dealerUSD × 0.71
msrpJOD = msrpUSD × 0.71

// Then summed across all items in the project:
equipmentDealerTotalJOD = Σ( dealerJOD × qty )
equipmentClientTotalJOD = Σ( msrpJOD × qty )
totalWeight = Σ( weight × qty ) // kg
Stage 2

Shipping Cost Calculation

The shipping cost has two components: a weight-based freight charge and three fixed logistics fees.

RATE_PER_KG = 4.50 JOD
CLEARANCE = 35 JOD
TRANSPORT = 70 JOD
DELIVERY_ORDER = 45 JOD

freightCost = totalWeight × 4.50
totalShippingCost = freightCost + 35 + 70 + 45

Example

Project with 120 kg total weight:

Freight (120 kg × 4.50)540.00 JOD
Clearance35.00 JOD
Transport70.00 JOD
Delivery Order45.00 JOD
Total Shipping690.00 JOD
Stage 3

Jordanian Customs & Taxes

The system applies eight Jordanian customs tax components to the import. The variable taxableAmount is the equipment dealer total in JOD (equipmentDealerTotalJOD).

Tax Code Description Formula Type
tax001 Import Duty taxableAmount × 5% % of value
tax020 VAT on Import (taxableAmount + tax001) × 16% % of value + duty
tax215 Services Tax taxableAmount × 1% % of value
tax301 Clearance Fee 50 JOD (fixed) Fixed
tax111 Shipping Insurance freightCost × 0.3% % of freight
tax016 Administrative Fee 23.20 JOD (fixed) Fixed
tax019 Processing Fee 25 JOD (fixed) Fixed
tax070 Municipality Tax taxableAmount × 5% % of value
Door-to-Door (Full):
doorToDoorCostJOD = totalShippingCost + all taxes including tax020
Used for profit reporting only.
Door-to-Door (excl. tax020):
doorToDoorCostExclTax020JOD = all taxes except tax020
Used in BOQ to prevent double-counting — tax020 is reflected via the client VAT at the end.

NogaHub · Void Quotation Guide Stages 4–5: Unit Pricing & Services
Stage 4 — The Core Pricing Formula

Per-Unit BOQ Price

Shipping and customs costs are not shown as a lump sum in the BOQ. Instead, they are distributed proportionally across every item based on that item's share of the total dealer cost. This produces a clean, per-unit "landed" price for the client.

// Step 1 — Compute cost-sharing ratios (once for the entire project)
shippingShare = totalShippingCost / equipmentDealerTotalJOD
customsShare = totalCustomsExclTax020 / equipmentDealerTotalJOD

// Step 2 — Per item allocation
shippingPerUnit = dealerJOD[item] × shippingShare
customsPerUnit = dealerJOD[item] × customsShare

// Step 3 — Final BOQ unit price (MSRP-based)
finalUnitPriceJOD = msrpJOD[item] + shippingPerUnit + customsPerUnit

// Step 4 — Line total
finalTotalJOD = finalUnitPriceJOD × quantity
Why MSRP, not dealer price? The client quotation is based on MSRP (the manufacturer's suggested retail price), not the wholesale dealer cost. This builds in equipment margin for NogaHub. Dealer cost is used only for internal profit calculations.

Worked Example — Single Item

Item: Void Cyclone 4 (IT1000) · Qty: 2  |  dealerUSD: $181.91 · msrpUSD: $330.75 · Weight: 1.3 kg each
Project totals (assumed): equipmentDealerTotal = 5,000 JOD · totalShipping = 690 JOD · totalCustoms (excl. 020) = 280 JOD

dealerJOD per unit181.91 × 0.71 = 129.16 JOD
msrpJOD per unit330.75 × 0.71 = 234.83 JOD
shippingShare690 / 5,000 = 0.1380
customsShare280 / 5,000 = 0.0560
shippingPerUnit129.16 × 0.1380 = 17.82 JOD
customsPerUnit129.16 × 0.0560 = 7.23 JOD
finalUnitPrice234.83 + 17.82 + 7.23 = 259.88 JOD
Line Total (×2)519.76 JOD

NogaHub · Void Quotation Guide Stages 6–7: Discount, VAT & Final Total
Stage 6

Global Discount

A single discount percentage set on the project is applied to the combined equipment + services subtotal after all per-unit prices are finalized.

// Stage 6 — Discount (App.js ~line 617)
subtotalBeforeDiscount = equipmentTotalJODBeforeDiscount
+ customEquipmentTotalJOD
+ servicesTotal

discountAmount = subtotalBeforeDiscount × (globalDiscount / 100)
projectSubtotalJOD = subtotalBeforeDiscount − discountAmount
Discount timing matters: The discount is applied to the combined subtotal (equipment + services). It is not applied line-by-line. Individual item prices shown in the BOQ already include their allocated shipping/customs, but the discount reduces the grand total proportionally.
Stage 7

VAT (16%) — Optional

Jordanian Value Added Tax (16%) is applied only when the project has includeTax = true. The tax is calculated on the discounted subtotal.

// Stage 7 — VAT (App.js ~line 623)
projectTaxJOD = includeTax ? projectSubtotalJOD × 0.16 : 0

projectTotalJOD = projectSubtotalJOD + projectTaxJOD
